    <h2>2. Description of the Product</h2>
    <p>
      <strong>NxtAbroad AI</strong> is an intelligent rules engine and scoring system that evaluates:
    </p>
    <ul>
      <li>Academic eligibility</li>
      <li>Financial capacity</li>
      <li>Documentation completeness</li>
      <li>Compliance with UKVI, IRCC, EU, and UAE study requirements</li>
      <li>Immigration risk indicators</li>
      <li>Visa readiness (Low / Medium / High)</li>
    </ul>
    <p>
      The engine has been deployed inside NxtAbroad’s operations where it supports staff in making consistent, accurate,
      and scalable decisions for student visa applicants. It evaluates hundreds of applications and forms the technological
      backbone of the organisation’s cross-border advisory operations.
    </p>
